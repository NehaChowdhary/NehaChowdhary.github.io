About Me

I'm a Software Engineer currently trying to get more in-depth knowledge of Computer Science by getting a hands-on experience in the same. To do this, I'm currently pursuing a Masters(MS) degree at Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur under the guidance of Prof. Sandip Chakraborty.

As a Researcher at UbiNET Lab , IIT Kharagpur, my research area is Operating Systems with focus on exploring Extended Berkeley Packet Filter (eBPF) and using it to research on observability in the Linux kernel with special focus on eBPF verifier. Being highly motivated to building my technical skill set and applying them to solve real life problems, I'm a result-oriented and self-motivated person looking for a challenging career where I can implement my knowledge to further enhance my skills.
